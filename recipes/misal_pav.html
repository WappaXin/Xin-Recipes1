Misal Pav

This recipe is of Kolhapuri variant, it is a spicy dish made of sprouts,potatoes,shev and a gravy of very thin consistency. This is accompinied by pav/bread slice/buns with lemon and onions. You can use yogurt/curd to cool it down as the siren goes off.
